Tres Cool

Tres Cool is a planet cooling - sustainable - digital public good. Available to all, without permission. Able to be sprinkled to every corner of the internet and set an entirely new standard of collective climate action. The carbon is removed from our atmosphere, tokenized and deposited back into the NFT - as an additional asset, at each and every trade. Increasing the fundamental value of the NFT and helping to cool our planet. This new standard can transform any NFT (e.g. Art, Collectible, Virtual Real Estate, In-Game Item) into a powerful tool to combat climate change and add a layer of additional value to holders.
